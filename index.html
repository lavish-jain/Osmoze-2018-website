Osmoze- Sharing Sapience Crossing Channels

What is Osmoze?

Osmoze is an amalgamation of Wisdom, Competition and Celebration of Chemical Engineering. With a motto of 'Sharing Sapience Crossing Channels', Osmoze is one of India's biggest festivals organised exclusively for Chemical Engineering Students.

The Hosts

Osmoze is hosted by the Department of Chemical Engineering and Technology, IIT (BHU), Varanasi: a Pioneer in the discipline with roots tracing back to almost a centuary.

About the Society

The Society of Chemical Engineers, lovingly called SoChem, is a student's organization under the patronage of the Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT (BHU), Varanasi. It acts as an interface between the students and the experienced faculty and administration of the department.

From HOD's Desk

It gives me immense pleasure to extend my warm welcome to you all on the behalf of our Chemical Engineering family. It fills me with immense pride to introduce you to the festival of Department of Chemical Engineering: OSMOZE. Organized as a student driven festival every year, the festival intends to display various ideas, aspects, progress and happenings in the field of Chemical engineering and technology. At the same time, OSMOZE brings out the hidden talents in upcoming chemical engineers. I believe the festival will help nurturing hopeful fresh faces filled with enthusiasm, shaping them into world-class technocrats by creating a perfect platform to enhance students' professional skills and experience a unique atmosphere of creativity and innovation. I look forward to see maximum support from you all. Students have shown great zeal and given their utmost effort in the development of this festival and have shown great enthusiasm for this three day extravaganza. I anticipate OSMOZE to scale ladders of success ahead with all your support. Thank you.
